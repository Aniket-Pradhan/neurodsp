    Filtering¶

    Design & Apply Filters¶

    filter_signal(sig, fs, pass_type, fc[, …]) Apply a bandpass, bandstop, highpass, or lowpass filter to a neural signal.
    filter_signal_fir(sig, fs, pass_type, fc[, …]) Apply an FIR filter to a signal.
    filter_signal_iir(sig, fs, pass_type, fc, …) Apply an IIR filter to a signal.
    design_fir_filter(sig_length, fs, pass_type, fc) Design an FIR filter.
    design_iir_filter(fs, pass_type, fc, …) Design an IIR filter.

    Filter Properties¶

    check_filter_definition(pass_type, fc) Check a filter definition for validity, and get f_lo and f_hi.
    check_filter_properties(b_vals, a_vals, fs, …) Check a filters properties, including pass band and transition band.
    compute_frequency_response(b_vals, a_vals, fs) Compute the frequency response of a filter.
    compute_pass_band(fs, pass_type, fc) Compute the pass bandwidth of a filter.
    compute_trans_band(f_db, db[, low, high]) Compute transition bandwidth of a filter.
    compute_nyquist(fs) Compute the nyquist frequency.

    Time-Frequency analysis¶

    phase_by_time(sig, fs, f_range[, …]) Calculate the phase time series of a neural oscillation.
    amp_by_time(sig, fs, f_range[, …]) Calculate the amplitude time series.
    freq_by_time(sig, fs, f_range[, …]) Estimate the instantaneous frequency at each sample.

    Spectral analysis¶

    compute_spectrum(sig, fs[, method, window, …]) Estimate the power spectral density (PSD) of a time series.
    compute_scv(sig, fs[, window, nperseg, …]) Compute the spectral coefficient of variation (SCV) at each frequency.
    compute_scv_rs(sig, fs[, window, nperseg, …]) Resampled version of scv: instead of a single estimate of mean and standard deviation, the spectrogram is resampled, either randomly (bootstrap) or time-stepped (rolling).
    spectral_hist(sig, fs[, window, nperseg, …]) Compute the distribution of log10 power at each frequency from the signal spectrogram.
    morlet_transform(sig, freqs, fs[, n_cycles, …]) Calculate the time-frequency representation of a signal using morlet wavelets.
    morlet_convolve(sig, freq, fs[, n_cycles, …]) Convolve a signal with a complex wavelet.
    rotate_powerlaw(f_axis, spectrum, delta_f[, …]) Change the power law exponent of a power spectrum about an axis frequency.

    Rhythmic analysis¶

    Burst Detection¶

    detect_bursts_dual_threshold(sig, fs, …[, …]) Detect periods of oscillatory bursting in a neural signal.
    compute_burst_stats(bursting, fs) Get statistics of bursts.

    Sliding Window Matching¶

    sliding_window_matching(sig, fs, win_len, …) Find recurring patterns in a time series using the sliding window matching algorithm.

    Lagged Coherence¶

    lagged_coherence(sig, f_range, fs[, …]) Quantify the rhythmicity of an oscillator using the lagged coherence measure.

    Signal simulation¶

    Periodic Signals¶

    sim_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq[, rdsym]) Simulate an oscillation.
    sim_bursty_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq[, …]) Simulate a bursty oscillation.
    sim_jittered_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq) Simulate a jittered oscillator, as defined by the oscillator frequency, the oscillator cycle, and how much (in time) to jitter each period.

    Aperiodic Signals¶

    sim_filtered_noise(n_seconds, fs[, …]) Simulate colored noise that is highpass or bandpass filtered.
    sim_synaptic_noise(n_seconds, fs[, …]) Simulate a neural signal with 1/f characteristics beyond a knee frequency.
    sim_ou_process(n_seconds, fs[, theta, mu, sigma]) Simulate mean-reverting random walk, as an Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process.
    sim_variable_powerlaw(n_seconds, fs[, exponent]) Generate a power law time series with specified exponent by spectrally rotating white noise.

    Combined Signals¶

    sim_noisy_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq[, …]) Simulate an oscillation embedded in background 1/f noise.
    sim_noisy_bursty_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq) Simulate a bursty oscillation embedded in background 1/f noise.
